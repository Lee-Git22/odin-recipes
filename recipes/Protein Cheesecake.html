Macro Friendly Protein Cheesecake by Josh Cortis

Description

The Macro Friendly Protein Cheesecake is a delicious dessert made from plain nonfat Greek yogurt to boost its protein content while keeping a low calorie count. Making it a delicious and healthy alternative to the classic cheesecake.


This recipe makes 8 servings, with each serving consisting of 136 calories, 0.4g of fat, 18g of carbs, and 15.6 of protein. Making it an excellent choice as a bodybuilding, healthy snack.

Ingredients

For the Crust

  • 3/4 cup (75g) protein pancake mix OR 3/4 cup of any flour with a few grams of splenda
  • 1/2 cup (113g) unsweetened applesauce

For the Filling

  • 3 cups (681g) plain nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 1 oz (30g) sugar free vanilla pudding mix
  • 2 tsp (8g) splenda
  • 13 tbsp (200g) liquid egg whites

For the Topping

  • 1 and 1/2 cups (200g) frozen fruit
  • 1 cup (64g) fat free whipped cream
  • 1/2 (2g) splenda
