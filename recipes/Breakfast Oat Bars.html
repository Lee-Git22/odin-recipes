Breakfast Oat Bars by Felu - Fit by cooking

Description

This 5 ingredient breakfast oat bar is super easy to make. Simplify your busy mornings by preparing these ahead of time. They are healthy and delicious as a snack too, perfect for people who are always on a move!

This recipe makes 6 servings at 205 calories, 20.3g carbs, 6.6g fat, and 14.8g protein


Ingredients

  • 150g oats
  • 30g cocoa
  • 60g CASEIN protein powder (Not Whey!)
  • 50g peanut butter
  • 150g milk (plus water if needed)
  • salt
