Breakfast Oat Bars by Felu - Fit by cooking

Description

This 5 ingredient breakfast oat bar is super easy to make. Simplify your busy mornings by preparing these ahead of time. They are healthy and delicious as a snack too, perfect for people who are always on a move!

This recipe makes 6 servings at 205 calories, 20.3g carbs, 6.6g fat, and 14.8g protein


Ingredients

  • 150g oats
  • 30g cocoa
  • 60g CASEIN protein powder (Not Whey!)
  • 50g peanut butter
  • 150g milk (plus water if needed)
  • salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees celsius
  2. Add 150g oats to a big mixing bowl
  3. Mix in 30g of cocoa powder
  4. Add 50g creamy peanut butter
  5. Mix in 60g of casein protein powder and a pinch of salt
  6. Finally add 150g milk and mix
  7. Mix until consistency is similar to nutella, adding in water if necessary
  8. Transfer mixed into baking dish lined in parchment paper
  9. Spread the mixture evenly
  10. Place into preheated oven for 25 minutes
  11. Remove and let the oat bar cool down for an additional 20 minutes to firm up
  12. Cut and serve into 6 oatbars

Enjoy!
